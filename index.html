How does Real Enquiries manage high volumes of enquiries?

Real Enquiries is designed to handle a high volume of enquiries efficiently. Through our integration with Daft, we collect and organize the enquiries into clients, properties, and more. This allows you to manage enquiries easily and maintain a streamlined workflow.

How does the Application HUB work?

Our modern Application HUB provides a platform for potential tenants to create an account, upload their profile and documents, and provide information about other occupants or guarantors. It also enables them to track their application status in real time, offering a user-friendly, intuitive experience.

What is the RTB integration and how does it benefit me?

The Residential Tenancies Board (RTB) integration in Real Enquiries allows you to easily check the registration status of your properties. It ensures that you always know which properties are regulated and registered with RTB and which ones are pending, keeping you compliant and informed at all times.

.

What benefits does the DocuSign integration provide?

With DocuSign integration, you can seamlessly generate leases, licenses, and addendums and send them for electronic signatures. This significantly speeds up the process, eliminates the need for physical paperwork, and ensures a smooth transition for your tenants.

What benefits does the Re-Leased integration provide?

With Re-Leased integration, you can seamlessly link the properties in your Real Enquiries account to your Re-Leased account. This allows you to easily manage your properties and tenancies in Re-Leased, while also managing the enquiries and applications in Real Enquiries.

Do I need to install any software to use Real Enquiries?

No, Real Enquiries is a Software as a Service (SaaS) solution. You don't need to install any software - simply log in from your browser on any device and get started.

How does Real Enquiries ensure my data is secure?

We take data security seriously. Real Enquiries is hosted on Amazon's reliable and secure services - EC2, RDS, SES, and S3. These platforms offer robust security measures, ensuring that your data is always protected.
