Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the basic types of questions for our customers. For more
information please contact us.

Email us your question
Q.

How does Real Enquiries manage my enquiries?

Real Enquiries integrates directly with Daft, funneling all your enquiries into one systematically organized place.

Q.

How does the tenant application process work?

Tenants can use our modern Application HUB to manage their application status in real time.

Q.

What is RTB integration?

RTB integration allows you to easily check the registration status of your properties, keeping you compliant and informed.

Q.

How does DocuSign integration streamline document management?

With our DocuSign integration, you can generate and send necessary documents like leases, licenses, and addendums with just a few clicks.

Q.

How does Re-Leased integration work?

By linking your Real Enquiries and Re-Leased accounts, you can manage properties and tenancies in Re-Leased while handling enquiries and applications in Real Enquiries.

Q.

Do I need to install any software to use Real Enquiries?

No, Real Enquiries is a SaaS solution. You can log in from your browser on any device.

Q.

How does Real Enquiries ensure data security?

Real Enquiries is hosted on Amazon's reliable and secure services. These platforms offer robust security measures, ensuring that your data is always protected.
